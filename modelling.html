Next we look into getting more insight about the highest (most prominent) peak on October 2020. To do this we do segmented regression by splitting the data to before and after October 2020. We do linear regression on the data before and the data after. First we look into the regression in the data before.

The p-value we get is 4.80e-5, with an RMSE of 1.64 and an R2 score of 0.44. With that, we say that for the data before October 2020, there is a significant relationship between the predictor and response.
Next let's look into the regression in the data after:

The p-value we get is 0.416, with an RMSE of 2.72 and an R2 score of 0.03. With that, we say that for the data after October 2020, there is no significant relationship between the predictor and response.
