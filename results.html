The higher number of smaller peaks after Oct. 2020 could explain the absence of a trend. Thus, we conclude that the Accusatory/Red-Tagging Tweets display a spiking pattern. This implies that there could be certain agents behind the disinformation. From what we see with the Oct. 2020 events, General Parlade red-tagging the celebrities led to our largest peak. This reinforces the definition of red-tagging as an act by State actors, and highlights how such actions contribute to the proliferation of disinformation, emphasizing the dangers associated with red-tagging.
